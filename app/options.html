You can specify tabs to automatically pin by regular expressions based on the URL. If you do not know what a regular expression is, simply put in the URL you want to automatically pin.
In the extension manager, click on "Configure Commands" to assign a keyboard shortcut (such as Alt-P) to the plugin to easily toggle pinned state.
You can specify http AND https with the same rule by using regular expression quantifiers. Example:
^http[s]{0,1}://www.facebook.com/$
